fundamentals

form collage

An initial, and perhaps my first, exploration of form and counterform. The task was to use a variety of letterforms and look at interesting aspects of a letter's form. Going a step further the forms of letters could be combined to make even more interesting and unique combinations.

publication

My final project in my Fundamentals course. In the assignment I had to choose between a list of designers and create a publication based on that designer's history and impact to the design world. The publication took the form of a foldable pamphlet to practice the different ways information can be displayed.
