letterforms

This project required a close examination of the characteristic shape and structure inherent within two distinctive letterforms. Continuing practicing with form and counterform, the task was to design visually interesting lettermarks by combining my initials.

wordplay

The task of this was to express the quality of the adjective, noun, or verb reinforcing the meaning. The excercise reinforced the idea how typography can "sing" and represent ideas through letting, tracking, kearning, and form without being pictoral.

grids

My first look into using a grid system and seeing the power that layout brings to a composition. Transitioning from expressive typography to information based the two can be combined to create visually interesting peices.
